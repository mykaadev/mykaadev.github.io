<p>My field of expertise is <strong>Tools Programming</strong> as well as <strong>UX and software design</strong>. I'm game design enthusiast and a passionate learner, always exploring new ideas.</p>
<p>I work on several hobby projects as a way to learn new stuff and often write my findings as <a href="{{ '/articles/' | relative_url }}" rel="nofollow">articles</a>. I'm always happy to share my knowledge and help others.</p>
